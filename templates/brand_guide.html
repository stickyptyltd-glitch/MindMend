Usage Guidelines

Do's
  • Maintain clear space around the logo
  • Use approved color variations only
  • Scale proportionally (lock aspect ratio)
  • Ensure sufficient contrast with background
  • Use SVG format when possible for crisp display
  • Keep logo legible at all sizes
Don'ts
  • Don't stretch or distort the logo
  • Don't change colors outside approved palette
  • Don't add effects, shadows, or outlines
  • Don't place on busy or conflicting backgrounds
  • Don't use below minimum size (120px width)
  • Don't rotate or modify the design elements
